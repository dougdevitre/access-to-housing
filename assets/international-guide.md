# International Market Adaptation Guide

Access to Housing is primarily designed for US markets, but the analytical frameworks are
adaptable to international contexts. This guide maps US-specific data sources and concepts
to their equivalents in other countries.

---

## Framework Compatibility

| Module | US-Specific? | International Adaptability |
|--------|:------------:|--------------------------|
| Module 1: Satellite Detection | No | Universal — satellite imagery works globally |
| Module 2: Permit Intelligence | Partially | Permit systems exist everywhere but vary in structure |
| Module 3: Supply Forecast | No | Framework works with any supply data |
| Module 4: Livability Score | Partially | Dimensions are universal; data sources differ |
| Module 5: Infrastructure Heatmap | No | Government investment data exists in most countries |
| Module 6: Climate Migration | No | Climate risk is global; migration data varies |
| Module 7: Opportunity Scanner | No | 5-factor scoring works for any market |
| Community Trust Pod | Partially | Civic systems vary but transparency principles are universal |

## Country-Specific Data Source Mapping

### United Kingdom

| US Source | UK Equivalent | URL | Notes |
|----------|--------------|-----|-------|
| Census ACS | ONS Census / Annual Population Survey | ons.gov.uk | Updated annually |
| FHFA HPI | UK HPI (HM Land Registry) | gov.uk/government/collections/uk-house-price-index | Monthly, by local authority |
| Census Building Permits | NHBC new home registrations | nhbc.co.uk | Quarterly |
| Zillow ZHVI | Rightmove / Zoopla indices | rightmove.co.uk/house-price-index | Monthly |
| Walk Score | Walk Score (available for UK) | walkscore.com | Limited coverage |
| First Street Foundation | Climate risk via Environment Agency | gov.uk/check-flood-risk | Flood only; fire/heat limited |
| FBI UCR | ONS Crime Survey | ons.gov.uk | Annual, by police force area |
| IRS SOI Migration | HMRC migration data / ONS internal migration | ons.gov.uk | Annual |
| FEMA FIRM | Environment Agency Flood Maps | flood-map-for-planning.service.gov.uk | Free |
| HUD Housing Counseling | Shelter / Citizens Advice | shelter.org.uk / citizensadvice.org.uk | Free advisory services |

**Fair Housing equivalent**: Equality Act 2010 — prohibits discrimination based on age, disability, gender reassignment, marriage, pregnancy, race, religion, sex, sexual orientation.

**Key differences**: Leasehold vs. freehold system, stamp duty instead of transfer tax, Section 21 eviction notices, Right to Buy for council housing.

### Canada

| US Source | Canadian Equivalent | URL | Notes |
|----------|-------------------|-----|-------|
| Census ACS | Statistics Canada Census / NHS | statcan.gc.ca | Every 5 years + annual surveys |
| FHFA HPI | Teranet–National Bank HPI / CREA HPI | housepriceindex.ca | Monthly |
| Census Building Permits | Statistics Canada Building Permits | statcan.gc.ca | Monthly, by CMA |
| Zillow ZHVI | CREA MLS HPI / Zolo | creastats.crea.ca | Monthly |
| Walk Score | Walk Score (available for Canada) | walkscore.com | Good coverage |
| First Street Foundation | Climate risk via NRCan | natural-resources.canada.ca | Flood/fire mapping |
| FBI UCR | Statistics Canada Uniform Crime Reporting | statcan.gc.ca | Annual |
| IRS SOI Migration | Statistics Canada mobility data | statcan.gc.ca | Census-based |
| FEMA FIRM | Federal flood mapping via NRCan | geoscan.nrcan.gc.ca | Provincial responsibility |
| HUD Housing Counseling | CMHC housing resources | cmhc-schl.gc.ca | National |

**Fair Housing equivalent**: Canadian Human Rights Act + provincial human rights codes — protected grounds vary by province but include race, national/ethnic origin, colour, religion, age, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, marital status, family status, disability.

**Key differences**: CMHC mortgage insurance (vs. FHA/VA), provincial rent control varies widely, First Nations reserve housing has separate governance, foreign buyer restrictions vary by province.

| US Source | Australian Equivalent | URL | Notes |
|----------|---------------------|-----|-------|
| Census ACS | ABS Census / Survey of Income and Housing | abs.gov.au | Every 5 years + annual |
| FHFA HPI | CoreLogic Home Value Index / ABS HPI | corelogic.com.au | Monthly |
| Census Building Permits | ABS Building Approvals | abs.gov.au | Monthly |

| FBI UCR | ABS Recorded Crime | abs.gov.au | Annual |
| IRS SOI Migration | ABS Internal Migration | abs.gov.au | Census-based |
| FEMA FIRM | State-level flood mapping | varies by state | No national equivalent |

**Key differences**: Negative gearing tax policy, stamp duty vs. land tax debate, state-based planning systems, Indigenous housing programs (separate governance), no equivalent to 30-year fixed mortgage (most mortgages are variable rate).

## Scoring Framework Adaptations

### Neighborhood Livability Score

The 4-dimension framework (Transit, Parks, Safety, Education) translates globally. Key adaptations:

| Dimension | US Approach | International Adaptation |
|-----------|------------|------------------------|
| Transit Access (0–25) | Walk Score + transit distance | Use local transit authority data; Walk Score available in UK/Canada/AU for major cities |
| Parks & Amenities (0–25) | TPL ParkScore + proximity | Use local parks authority data; grocery/healthcare proximity is universal |
| Safety Indicators (0–25) | FBI UCR density-normalized | Use national crime statistics office; always density-normalize |
| Education Access (0–25) | Proximity within 1.5 mi | Adjust radius for local norms; proximity only, never test scores |

### Displacement Early Warning

The 4-dimension framework translates with these source substitutions:

| Dimension | US Source | International Substitute |
|-----------|----------|------------------------|
| Affordability Pressure | Census ACS rent burden | National statistics office housing cost data |
| Tenure Instability | Eviction Lab | Court records, housing tribunal data, or NGO tracking |
| Investment Pressure | Property records | Land registry transaction data |
| Policy Protection | Local ordinance review | National/local tenant protection legislation review |

### Government Accountability Scorecard

Civic systems vary significantly. Adapt the framework:

| Dimension | US Approach | International Notes |
|-----------|------------|-------------------|
| Code Enforcement | Municipal code enforcement records | Building inspection authority data; may be national or local |
| Permit Processing | Building department records | Planning authority records; timelines vary (UK: 8–13 weeks statutory) |
| Complaint Resolution | 311 system data | Varies — some countries use centralized complaint portals, others don't |
| Public Accessibility | Meeting access review | Parliamentary/council meeting access; FOIA equivalent varies by country |

## FOIA Equivalents by Country

| Country | Law | Key Differences |
|---------|-----|-----------------|
| **US** | Freedom of Information Act (1966) | Federal + state laws; 20 business day response |
| **UK** | Freedom of Information Act (2000) | 20 working day response; £600 cost limit |
| **Canada** | Access to Information Act (1983) | 30 calendar day response; federal + provincial laws |
| **Australia** | Freedom of Information Act (1982) | 30 day response; federal + state laws |
| **EU** | Regulation 1049/2001 + national laws | Varies by member state; GDPR intersects |
| **New Zealand** | Official Information Act (1982) | 20 working day response; considered gold standard |
